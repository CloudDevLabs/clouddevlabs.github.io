ABOUT US

Future-proof software using future-proof preccesses

CloudDevLabs is not just yet another custom software development company that promisses you to deliver code as a result of a successful project.

We cater our clients with a Holistic appproach, which not only means to leverage the power of scalable architectures

We are strong believers in:

  • Agile methoddologies and XP practices
  • Cross-functional, self-organized, empowered product teams
  • DevOps culture
  • Product-centric / Lean approach

With headquarters in Toronto, ON, Canada and most of our talented teams operating from Lviv, Ukraine, CloudDevLabs provides a unique combination of Canadian/US experince and deep proficiency in technology and processes, while saving costs.
